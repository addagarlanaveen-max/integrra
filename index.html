About Integgra

Structured assurance for food businesses that need clarity, consistency and confidence.

Integgra is an assurance & compliance partner supporting food businesses through structured systems, audits and operational support.

We help organizations build stronger food safety and compliance cultures with practical, resilient and thoughtfully designed systems.

Services

Quality assurance that works on the factory floor.

Advisory, documentation, training, and audit support designed around your actual products, people, layouts, hazards, and buyer requirements.

Food Safety Audits

GMP, GHP, PRP, sanitation, allergen, traceability, pest, storage, and process control assessments.

HACCP & Risk Control

FSMS Documentation
